Welcome! to Mead Help?

What is Mead?

Mead is a Honey wine, which tends to be quite strong and alcoholic-tasting, as honey ferments very well. Mead is often made with other ingredients for flavour, be they fruit or grain.

Our Ambition

MeadHelp? is designed to give individuals from ages 21 and up, the knowledge and inspiration they desire to create a Mead of their choice. This is accomplished by using the MeadHelp video guides and the MeadHelp? application. The Meadhelp? app allows a user to explore different possible recipes, and inspire him or her, if the individual is uncertain of what ingredients to use.
